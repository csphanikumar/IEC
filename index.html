The deadline for submitting IEC applications for the upcoming round is the 5th of each month.

The IEC meeting for that round will be held in the last week of the same month.

Overview

The IIT Delhi Institute Ethics Committee (IEC) reviews and approves research projects involving human subjects, but only if they are conducted by IIT Delhi faculty. The IEC exists to ensure that research follows ethical standards, as individual researchers may not always make the right decisions.

Many past cases highlight the need for ethical oversight in medical and academic research.The IEC office will announce upcoming meetings one month in advance. IIT Delhi faculty must submit a signed hard copy of their application, along with the required documents, to the IEC office at Block IV, 3rd Floor, Room 4B9. Additionally, they must email a complete application form and documents as a single PDF to iitd_iec@iitd.ac.in.

After submission, the application will go through a technical and ethical review. Any feedback will be sent to the faculty member or principal investigator.If needed, the researcher may have to appear before the committee to address concerns. Students or postdocs cannot submit or present the application on behalf of a faculty member.
